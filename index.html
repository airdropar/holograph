Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

No
Type
01
What is Holograph?

Holograph is the most advanced, accessible and affordable AR software in the world. Our technology enables brands to make any surface or space interactive, for audiences to unlock with their smartphones of today and wearables of tomorrow.

02
What is Augmented Reality (AR)?

Augmented reality (AR) overlays the physical world with digital content, viewable through smartphones and glasses.

03
How Does Holograph Work?

Our technology includes three main components: (1) The Spatial Editor to build AR scenes. (2) The Spatial Publisher to distribute AR scenes onto physical spaces and surfaces in the real world. (3) The Spatial Entry Points for audiences to unlock the AR scenes with codes and links, without an app.

04
What are AR Codes & AR Links?

AR Codes and AR Links allow audiences to unlock brand activations without an app. Scanning an AR Code and clicking an AR Link launch spatial media experiences immediately.

05
How long does it take to augment a physical surface or space?

Our AR technology offers real-time publishing so that once content is ready, your project can go live immediately.

Content production varies per project, but we recommend allocating 1-2 weeks.
